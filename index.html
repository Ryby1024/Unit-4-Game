Defeat Thanos

How To Play

The Avengers need your help!!

Thanos is trying to regain the Infinity Stones and only you can help the Avengers defeat him.

Each Avenger represents a numerical value when clicked. The goal is to equal the score that Thanos has without going over.

If you can match Thanos' score then you and the Avengers save the world.

Be careful though, if you go over by just one point he wins and regains the stones so chose wisely.
